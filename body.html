7-Day Exercise Plan
Day 1: Push Ups
30 push ups: Builds chest, triceps, and core.
Start slow, keep elbows close to body.
Day 2: Squats
40 squats: Strengthens legs and glutes.
Keep your knees in line with toes.
Day 3: Plank
3 x 30s planks: Core stability and posture.
Hold your back & hips straight.
Day 4: Mountain Climbers
3 x 25s sets: Cardio and core.
Quick knees, keep breathing!
Day 5: Jumping Jacks
50 reps: Full body warm up.
Arms and legs move in-sync.
Day 6: Lunges
30 lunges: Strengthens thighs/glutes.
Step forward, lower until knees bent at 90°.
Day 7: Brisk Walk / Yoga
20–30 minutes: Active rest; choose a brisk walk, or light yoga stretches.
Helps recovery, flexibility, reduces stress.
